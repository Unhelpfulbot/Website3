Central Park

Central Park is a located in the center of Manhattan giving the name Central, as Manhattan is the center of business in New York. Central Park is around 1.32 square miles. There are beautiful sights and gigantic rocks located throughout the park. At certain locations of the park there are street performers displaying their talents. It is a great place to enjoy nature.
